Kaya · Module Proposal v1 · 9 May 2026
Phase 1.6 · The Pantry (Soko)

Run the household.

Where parents — with helpers, optionally with kids — plan meals, manage the master shopping list, track the food & household budget, and one-tap send the run to suppliers over WhatsApp. Stand-alone module. Not linked to the Hive.

01

Six surfaces under one module

Each surface owns its own ritual. They share data — staples flow into the weekly list, meals link to ingredients, suppliers tag every item. The Pantry tab bar at the bottom of every screen jumps you across.

01 /pantry

Home

Dashboard for the week. Budget bar, active list preview, upcoming meals, "Send to suppliers" CTA.

🛒
02 /pantry/staples

Staples

Master list of recurring items. Default qty, last-bought price, supplier hint. Source of truth for every weekly list.

📦
03 /pantry/list/[id]

List

Active shopping run. Auto-populated from Staples. Grouped by supplier; one-tap WhatsApp send per supplier.

📝
04 /pantry/meals

Meals

7-day food timetable. Breakfast, lunch, dinner, eating-out. Phase 2: meals → ingredients → list.

🍽️
05 /pantry/budget

Budget

Monthly category budgets. Live spend. "TSh 12k left · 8 days remaining" footers everywhere.

💰
06 /pantry/suppliers

Soko · Suppliers

Trusted vendors with WhatsApp numbers. Filtered window onto The Roster — same record, two views.

🏪
02

The screens

Mobile-first. Tab bar pinned at the bottom. Same color language as the rest of Kaya, leaf-green accent so the section feels its own.

03

Who can do what

Full access

Parents

  • Edit staples, lists, budgets
  • Add & edit suppliers
  • Send WhatsApp orders
  • Toggle kid-suggestion mode
Operational

Helpers

  • Edit lists, check items off
  • Edit staples
  • Send WhatsApp orders
  • No budget edits · parents own the money
Read + suggest

Kids

  • See lists, meals, what's coming
  • Suggest items (parent confirms)
  • No edit, no send, no budget
  • Toggleable per family
04

Roadmap

PHASE 1A · NOW

The shopping run

Get a household's weekly run from spreadsheet to one-tap. No budget yet, no meal plan.

  • Staples master list
  • Active list grouped by supplier
  • Suppliers shortlist (Soko view)
  • WhatsApp send-list deep links
  • Pantry Home dashboard preview
PHASE 1B · NEXT

Plan + track

Add the parts that turn it into a habit: budgets and meal planning.

  • Meals planner (7-day grid)
  • Monthly category budgets
  • Sunday auto-populate
  • Budget warnings + insights
PHASE 2 · FUTURE

Automate the run

Pricing intelligence, recipe → list, payments, delivery, supplier-side onboarding.

  • Location-based price suggestions
  • Recipe → ingredients → list
  • Credit-card-on-file orders
  • Supplier sign-up + Roster bridge

Final approval call.

If the six surfaces, permissions and phasing feel right, say the word and PR-Pantry-A.1 starts: schema (designed for Soko↔Roster filtering from day one), security rules, PantryContext, the four Phase-1A surfaces, and WhatsApp send. Phase 1B follows on a stack.
