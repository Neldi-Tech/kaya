Phase 1.5 · The Hive

Three layers. One Hive. Real cash, on-hand or safekept.

v3 splits the Cash layer into On Hand (the kid's pocket) and On Deposit (parent safekeeping), introduces the Safekeeping screen, adds a destination picker to cash-out, and exposes the hooks for Kaya Business sale & cost approvals. Currency is family-configured, never hard-coded.

01

The 3-Layer Model

Each layer has a different purpose. House Points are flexible (in-family rewards, screen time, privileges). Honey Coins are intentional savings (a deliberate "I'm setting this aside"). Cash is real-world money the kid can receive and spend with parent approval. Two parent-controlled rates govern the flow.

03

Safekeeping · On Hand ↔ On Deposit v3 · NEW

Real cash splits two ways. The kid carries some — that's On Hand. The rest is handed to a parent for safekeeping — that's On Deposit. Two atomic flows: the kid logs a deposit when handing physical cash over (parent confirms), and the parent issues a withdrawal when handing physical cash back. Mirrors how a real bank account works — the lesson is built in.

05

Updated Roadmap

PHASE 1.5 · NOW (v3)

The Hive · Cash split

Three layers, two rates, full Wallet — with Cash now split into On Hand and On Deposit, plus the Safekeeping flow.

  • HP ↔ Honey ↔ Cash (with destination picker)
  • Wallet · Cash In · Cash Out · Safekeeping
  • Parent approval on spend & sales (per-kid threshold)
  • Family-configured currency (TZS / USD / …)
PHASE 2 · ALONGSIDE

Kaya Business 🌱

Each kid runs a real micro-business. Sales credit Hive Cash; costs draw from a parent-funded float or the kid's wallet. Sunday auto-generated weekly P&L visible to siblings.

  • 5 kid screens: My Business · Assets · Sales · Costs · Report
  • Family Price List + Parent Console
  • Care reminders → House Points via Hive routines
  • Asset stages, lifecycle, loss tracking
PHASE 3 · NEXT

Real-money rails

Connect Cash to real bank / mobile-money rails so On Hand and On Deposit can sync to actual accounts.

  • M-Pesa / Tigo Pesa / Stripe
  • Auto-drip allowance
  • Real-bank-backed safekeeping
  • Donation partners · spending receipts
  • Optional global-profile USD translation

v3 sign-off.

The Hive v3 (cash split + safekeeping + sale plumbing) is the upstream that lets Kaya Business plug in cleanly. The matching code prompt has been updated to v3 — see Kaya-Hive_Code-Prompt_2026-05-07.md (updated 2026-05-13) for the schema migration, new Cloud Functions, and acceptance criteria.
