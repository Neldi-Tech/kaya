What's new: the NAVIGATE box with two big tap targets — ← Back (cream button, calls router.back()) and 🏠 Kaya Home (chocolate button, jumps straight to /dashboard). It sits inline with content, just before the section tab bar at the bottom. No need to scroll back up to use the top-header chevron.

  • Whether to also show on desktop (currently mobile-only since the desktop sidebar already has both affordances)
  • Whether to show on every non-home page or only deep modules
