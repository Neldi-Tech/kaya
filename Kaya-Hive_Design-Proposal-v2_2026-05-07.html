01

The 3-Layer Model

Each layer has a different purpose. House Points are flexible (in-family rewards, screen time, privileges). Honey Coins are intentional savings (a deliberate "I'm setting this aside"). Cash is real-world money the kid can receive and spend with parent approval. Two parent-controlled rates govern the flow.

Updated Roadmap

PHASE 1 · NOW

The Hive

Three layers, two rates, full Wallet. Goals, Insights, Quests on top.

  • House Points ↔ Honey Coins ↔ Cash
  • Wallet · Cash In · Cash Out
  • Goals & behavior insights
  • Parent approval on cash spend
PHASE 2 · NEXT

Real-money rails

Connect cash balance to real bank/mobile-money rails (M-Pesa, Tigo Pesa, Stripe).

  • Auto-drip allowance
  • Cash-out to mobile money
  • Donation partners
  • Spending receipts
PHASE 3 · FUTURE

The Beehive · Business

Each kid runs a real micro-enterprise. Income flows directly into Cash.

  • Business income line
  • Cost & profit P&L
  • Inventory & sales log
  • Reinvest vs. save split
