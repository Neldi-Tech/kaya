FIX 2Your approval sends it straight to budget

Reconciling — entering the real shopping amount — only makes sense for things a helper goes and buys. A fixed salary or an auto top-up has nothing to reconcile, yet today you have to close it out on their behalf. FIX 3May's pay → May's budget (paid early June)

The screenshot showed June's salary before June arrived, because pay falls on the 1st–5th of the following month and the budget followed the payment date. We flip it: the salary belongs to the work month. May's salary is approved & booked into May's budget a few days before month-end, sits as Processing, then your single tap in the pay window just records that it's paid.
