03How a Stopped cost is counted rev 2

Your point: stopping shouldn't always wipe the cost — if you prepaid for the year, that money's already gone, so it should keep counting until the year you paid for runs out. If you were paying month-to-month, stopping ends the charges, so it drops off straight away.

● Active / Trial
Counts fully — exactly as today. Sits in Active count, Monthly equivalent & Annual.
⏸ On hold
Drops out while held — you're not paying during a hold; reminders pause. Snaps back in when you Resume.
⏹ Stopped · monthly
Drops out immediately. Monthly / weekly / daily billing = no more charges the moment you stop.
Monthly −Annual −
⏹ Stopped · prepaid
Keeps counting until paid-through. Annual / semi-annual / quarterly that's already paid stays in Monthly equivalent & Annual until its paid-through date passes — then it drops off automatically.
Monthly ✓ till paid-throughAnnual ✓
Worked example. You stop FT Digital (TZS 240k/yr, paid through Mar 2027) today, 7 Jun 2026. Because it's prepaid, its 20k/mo equivalent stays in your Monthly & Annual figures until Mar 2027, then auto-drops. Stop City Gym (60k/mo) and it leaves the totals immediately — next month is 60k lighter.

PlatformiOS · Android · Web/SaaS · Other chip row + a “By platform” breakdown card (count & monthly each), shown for the Mobile Apps & Software category. Each app row carries a small platform badge.
Status filterAll · Active · On hold · Stopped chip row (with counts), beside the existing Paid-by & Category filters. Default = Active. “All” shows everything in one list.
Hero totalsAlways show Active count · Monthly equivalent · Annual at live commitment — never affected by which status filter is on.
HoldStatus → paused. Drops out of all totals; reminders & post-due check pause; optional auto-resume date; one-tap Resume.
Stop · monthlyStatus → cancelled + endedOn stamped. Leaves Monthly & Annual immediately (pay-as-you-go ends).
Stop · prepaidStatus → cancelled but stays in Monthly & Annual until the paid-through date (next renewal you already paid for), then auto-drops. Applies to quarterly / semi-annual / annual.
Kept safeCycles, payments & receipts untouched — status only changes what's counted going forward (“history is sacred”).
