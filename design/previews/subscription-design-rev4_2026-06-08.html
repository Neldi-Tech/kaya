Why paid-via first?

Every subscription is billed somewhere — your Apple ID, your Google/Play account, or a card on the web. That's the line you actually reconcile against. So Paid-via becomes the top axis:

📱 iOSAnything on the Apple ID / App Store bill.
🤖 AndroidGoogle Play / Android billing.
🌐 WebPaid directly on a site / by card.
📦 OtherCash, bank order, in-person (gym, club).

Category (Streaming, Cloud, Membership…) and the existing details stay — they're just the second line now, under Paid-via.
