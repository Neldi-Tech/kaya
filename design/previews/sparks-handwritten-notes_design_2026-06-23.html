What changes: the weekly post gains a “Handwritten this week” rail of the actual scanned pages, and every highlight quote shows the thumbnail of the page it came from (tap → full-screen lightbox). The transcribed text stays — we just put the real handwriting next to it so the post genuinely shows the notes, not only the typed version.

Same treatment added to the daily entry’s “recent days” list.

What changes: mirrors the Home-Projects pattern (coral→green % bar) and the Revisions AI score. Kaya (AI) auto-scores one dimension — soundness of the reflection (depth/honesty/completeness). Parents score two: soundness and handwriting, on 0–100 sliders. Stars/notes stay optional. Read-only for kids, with the usual “AI can be wrong” caveat.

What changes: after the existing page-detect + flatten + clean steps, a new content-trim pass measures the ink bounding-box and tightens the crop to the writing (with a small margin) — killing the empty lower half. The Adjust crop editor (draggable corners, already built) is surfaced so a kid/parent can nudge it. A stronger Clean B&W mode makes faint pencil read crisply.
