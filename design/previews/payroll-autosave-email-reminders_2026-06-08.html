Auto-save the payroll setup

Every control saves the moment you change it (debounced ~½ s) with a live “Saving… / ✓ Saved” chip. No more remembering to tap Update — that's why changes were getting lost. Invalid fields (e.g. rate = 0) show inline and just don't save that one field.

The 7-days-before email

When Kaya raises the month's salaries (~7 days before month-end), it emails your chosen inboxes — so you're told even if you don't open the app.

💰 May salaries are ready
Hi Elia,

Kaya raised 3 salaries for May 2026 and booked them to May's budget as Processing:
• Catherine — TZS 100,000
• Seif — TZS 322,000
• Donald — TZS 572,000

Total: TZS 994,000. Pay window: 1–5 June. Tap below to review & mark paid.
Open Payroll →
Sent to elia@eafoods.com, accounts@eafoods.com · manage in Settings → Notifications
⚙️ What makes this automatic: a tiny daily server job (cron) checks who's due ~7 days before month-end, raises the salaries, and sends this email. Today the raise only happens when you open the Payroll page — the cron makes it truly hands-off. (Email no-ops safely until the Resend key is set.)
✨ Extras I'd add: a “Mark paid” reminder email on the 1st of the pay window, a one-tap “Send test email”, and an optional monthly payroll digest (everything paid + upcoming) to accounts@.
