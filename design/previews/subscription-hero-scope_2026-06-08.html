How the numbers compute
Follows the filterThe three hero cards recompute over exactly what's in view — the active Paid-via + Status (+ category) filters. Tap iOS → iOS totals; tap Android → Android totals.
Scope chipA chip right above the cards always names the scope (“📱 iOS · Active”, “⏸ On hold · Everyone”) with a one-tap ✕ to clear back to All — so the numbers are never ambiguous.
Labels adaptFor Active: This-month-due · Monthly equiv · Annualized (as today). For On hold: count · “Paused /mo (if resumed)” · per-year. For Stopped: count · what dropped off · prepaid still-counting. So each view reads naturally.
Default unchangedOn “All”, the cards show the whole picture exactly like today — the gold highlight + delta (“48% of total”) only appear once you've narrowed.
Gold highlightWhen filtered, the cards get a subtle gold edge + a “of 182k / 48% of total” delta so you instantly see this slice vs the whole.
